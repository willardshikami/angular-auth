About

healthcare ecosystem

Healthix Solutions is a healthcare technology business working to enable a vibrant Healthcare Ecosystem - connecting Patients, Payers, Providers, Pharmaceutical companies, Governments and other stakeholders with a focus to improve patient care. Healthix platform is a shared exchange - seamless exchange services between all healthcare players in the Healthcare Ecosystem - platform that connects once siloed/stand-alone services into integrated networks. Our connected platform provides more value to the different stakeholders especially in the roadmap to achieving their digital strategy.

  • RESOURCE SHARING

    Allows resource sharing amongst the healthcare players without increasing their costs.

  • SIMPLE TOOLS

    Provides simple tools to providers to upload information to the payers.

  • TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT

    Enables training and development to personnel in provider and payer institutions.

  • CONNECTIVITY & ACCESS

    Provides connectivity & access for payers and providers in the Healthcare ecosystem.

  • PATIENT MANAGEMENT

    Provides better patient management for the providers; patient validation tools are available to them

  • ONLINE CLAIM TOOL

    Is a robust online claim reconciliation tool with payment enabled.

  • REDUCED COSTS

    Reduces on the operational costs to payer and provider institutions.
